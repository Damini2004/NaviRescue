Documentation


Traffic accidents remain a significant public health concern, often caused by driver inattention, negligence, or lack of awareness about traffic rules. Existing infrastructure may not adequately address these issues, leading to injuries, fatalities, and significant economic costs.

Solution: Develop and implement a comprehensive traffic safety project by integrating cameras and LCD screens on traffic signals to minimize the accident rate by creating awareness amongst the public.

This project mainly focuses on creating awareness amongst the public by using various innovative 3D strategies, including displaying Traffic Safety Awareness like displaying dynamic messages on the LCD screen covering various topics like Recent traffic rule updates Safe driving practices (e.g., maintaining distance, avoiding distractions), etc.

It will Capture and Display live violence of rules done by citizens as The camera captures images or videos of rule violations (e.g., red light running, zebra crossing violation).

The microcontroller analyzes captured data and identifies potential violations. For confirmed violations, display a snippet of the captured image/video on the LCD screen momentarily along with the specific rule broken and potential fine amount. This serves as a real-time deterrent and reinforces the consequences of breaking traffic rules.

It will also include integrated sensors at zebra crossings (e.g., pressure pads, infrared beams) to detect pedestrian presence. When a vehicle disobeys the zebra crossing rules (e.g., enters while a pedestrian is present), the buzzer activates, alerting both the driver and the pedestrian.

A similar buzzer system can be implemented for critical areas like no-stopping zones or turn lanes.

This refined system, with its intelligent data processing, visual and audible warnings, and focus on public awareness, holds the potential to revolutionize traffic safety.

Remember, its success hinges on responsible data practices, legal compliance, and ongoing public engagement.
